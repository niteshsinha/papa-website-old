About

Lucknow based photographer, evolved with Art, Culture & Theatre Photography for more than four decades. In this span prepared a vast & valuable archives of photographs of Great Indian Classical Musicians, Dancers capturing their memorable moods in their live concerts. Exhibited several times B/W enlargements in various music & dance festivals by important organizations, banners & Govt. academics. Well acclaimed by press & media. An active photojournalist. Awarded Rashtriya Yuva Prathisthan Award for cultural photography. UP Sangeet Natak Academy Award for Cultural Photography (2007) . Sanskar Bharti’s Samman and Bharatyam’s Kala Award.
